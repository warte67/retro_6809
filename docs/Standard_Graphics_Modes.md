# Standard Graphics modes:

## Standard Text Modes:

| MODE | WIDTH | HEIGHT | COLUMNS |  ROWS  | BUFFER |
|:----:|:-----:|:------:|:-------:|:------:|:------:|
$00 | 320 | 200 | 40 | 25 | 2000 |
$01 | 256 | 160 | 32 | 20 | 1280 |
$02 | 320 | 100 | 40 | 12 | 960 |
$03 | 256 | 80 | 32 | 10 | 640 |
$04 | 160 | 200 | 20 | 25 | 1000 |
$05 | 128 | 160 | 16 | 20 | 640 |
$06 | 160 | 100 | 20 | 12 | 480 |
$07 | 128 | 80 | 16 | 10 | 320 |
